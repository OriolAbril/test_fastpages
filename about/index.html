About Me

I’m a renegade physicist/engineer who loves statistical data analysis. By day, I am currently a Research Assistant on Bayesian Model Selection with David Rossell at UPF, Barcelona. By night I am a core developer of ArviZ a Python package for exploratory analysis of Bayesian models. In addition to data analysis, I also love programming and teaching. I am also interested in open science and reproducible research.

In my spare time, I like to play board games and going to the beach to do water activities. I have been sailing and snorkeling regularly since I was little and more I recently added kayaking to the mix too! I generally spend the summer at the Costa Brava.
